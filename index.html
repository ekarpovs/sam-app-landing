Welcome to SamApp

Track pets, feeds & foods across devices with ease.

SamApp is a lightweight, cross-device app for managing pet feeding routines. It helps households track meals, portions, and feeding history — whether you're the owner or a family member.

Key Terms

Term Meaning
Client A household or group managing pets together
Owner The person who registers the client and manages setup
Member Anyone in the household who logs in to feed pets
Pet An animal being tracked in SamApp
Meal A scheduled feeding event for a pet
Portion The amount of food given during a meal
